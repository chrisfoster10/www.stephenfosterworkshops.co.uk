OIL AND ACRYLIC  WORKSHOPS with Stephen Foster B.A.

 

At Emery Down Village Hall SO43 7EB (near Lyndhurst)

 

Each one-day workshop emphasises palette knife technique and includes demonstrations.  Above all a spontaneous and “loose” approach is encouraged, generally using a rather limited palette.

 

Each workshop will start with a demonstration. Every subject can be interpreted in many different ways so I am very happy if you wish to work in a more abstract way or from your own material.

 

New Feature: Bring your problem paintings and I will discuss them (publicly or privately) after each workshop.

 

Numbers are limited to a maximum of eleven for each workshop.
