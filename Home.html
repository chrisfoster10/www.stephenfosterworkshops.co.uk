OIL & ACRYIC WORKSHOPS

with

Stephen Foster B.A.

 
 

At Emery Down Village Hall SO43 7EB (near Lyndhurst)

Each one-day workshop emphasises palette knife technique for Acrylic and Oil painters and includes demonstrations.  Above all a spontaneous and “loose” approach is encouraged, generally using a rather limited range of colours.

 Numbers are limited to a maximum of eleven for each workshop.

Price £38 per workshop

A 16” x 16” prepared board is supplied free to each painter.
